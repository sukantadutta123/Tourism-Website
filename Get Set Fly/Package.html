Seas

The most beautiful blue spread of eastern oceans. Famous for serene, white sandy beaches and delicious sea food of port blair.

Goa

Goa, a coastal paradise in India, entices with its pristine beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese influence, water sports, spice plantations, and a delightful blend of Indian and Western cultures.

Shady yet bright experience of Austrailian seas. Highly popular for spearfishing, with the premier target species being the elusive red morwong.

Landscapes

Kerala

Kerala, a coastal state in India, is renowned for its tranquil backwaters, lush greenery, and rich cultural heritage. It offers stunning landscapes, vibrant festivals, Ayurvedic traditions, and delectable cuisine.

Iceland

Land of the vikings and beautiful waterfalls, also widely known as “The Land of Fire and Ice”.Iceland is home to some of the largest glaciers in Europe.

Sikkim

Sikkim, a scenic jewel in northeastern India, offers breathtaking landscapes, snow-capped peaks, vibrant monasteries, trekking trails, and a rich cultural heritage. Explore its beauty and find tranquility in nature.
